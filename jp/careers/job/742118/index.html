Solution Architect

Permanent employee, full-time - Norway, Oslo
The team and your role
We are looking for a new Solution Architect to expand our design and pre-sales team. 
You will be responsible for designing, planning and delivering projects on a Service Development Platform. 
You will be regarded as an expert in your domain, knowing more acronyms and 3GPP references than you wish, and making jokes about it with other telco-minded individuals in our organization. You will thrive in architecting and delivering telco services differently, 
on a public cloud infrastructure.  

You will become part of a small team with another solution architect and sales engineers. 
The team spans across the world and across the organization, supporting sales, engineering and delivery. 
It is key for the successful delivery of our services to new customers.

From your education and hands-on experience, you are not afraid to dive down the layers.
If you are confident in your technical decisions, eager to learn on a daily basis and ready to design innovative telco solutions,
now is the time to join our team!

We are a remote friendly company, all team members are currently within 5+ hours of the CET timezone (Norway, Sweden, France, Japan)

You'll enjoy

  • Being part of a dynamic team of telco engineers, experts in their domain
  • Working closely with sales, pre-sales, software engineers and management
  • The opportunity to improve your thought leadership and technical architecture skills
  • Taking part in all the lifecycle of a project, from initial customer discussion to go-live
  • Having direct influence on the success of those projects
  • Career and professional development opportunities

What you will be doing

  • Analyzing customer requirements (technical, business) and map those to internal functionalities
  • Identify key issues and solutions
  • Architecture design (Contract, HLD, LLD)
  • Participating in system integration and development projects
  • Preparing solution description, technical specifications and project documentation
  • Principal point of contact for all technical discussions with the customer
  • Building and maintaining customer technical relationships
  • Supporting the existing team in maintaining complex platforms for telco operators, solving difficult and complex technical issues
 
We expect you to bring along

  • A positive and creative mindset, ability to identify issues and come up with innovative solutions.
  • Solid experience from the Telco industry in a similar position (e.g. Customer Lead, Integration Manager, Technical Sales Architect, Solution Architect). You may also have worked as a Core Network engineer/Systems engineer for several years, and looking for a change.
  • Delivered and operated 3GPP mobile core networks (GSM, UMTS, LTE, IMS) to Tier 1 or Tier 2 MNOs, preferably in different countries.
  • Deep understanding of telecommunication signaling protocols, such as SIP, Diameter, Sigtran, GTP. 
  • Understand IP networking principles (VLAN, BGP, FW)
  • Experience in scripting or software development (Java, Python)
  • Familiar with cloud technologies (containerization, orchestration, infrastructure-as-code) and tools (Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform)
  • Assertiveness, self-confidence, and customer-facing skills
  • Fluent in English

It's a plus if you have

  • Knowledge of country-specific regulatory compliance (security, LI, emergency, …)
  • Know at least one telco acronym that has different meanings depending on the context
  • Pre-sales experience, answered to RFI/P
  • Familiar with 5G SA architecture
  • Experience with AWS
  • Foreign language
  • Familiar with RAN planning and integration

 Our company culture
We’re a challenger in a well established multi-billion dollar industry. To succeed as a company, we need to deliver the high quality core services that subscribers are used to, while also adding value through the way that we operate. We believe our people and our culture will be a key differentiator in achieving this.

If you join us, you’ll get the trust and freedom needed to make a big impact in the telco industry. Since we’re solving telco problems in a modern way, we recruit from various industries and have people that used to work at Telenor, Google, Microsoft, Spotify, and AWS. We promise that you’ll be surrounded by friendly, motivated and highly skilled coworkers. 

We have a culture of transparency and openness, and oftentimes we call ourselves a “start-up for grown-ups”. We know where we’re going, and with a lot of trust in each other, we’ll pave the way there together.

We’re a distributed company with offices in Oslo, Trondheim and Stockholm, as well as some remote colleagues in other locations (a total of 10 countries). We’ll pay for your phone and internet, as well as your lunch and office snacks. We’ll even pay you for your work! 

The Covid-19 pandemic forced a lot of companies to change how they think, but for us it confirmed that our remote-friendly and transparent approach goes a long way. We frequently host company wide activities online, like tech talks, demos, and failure fridays. 

We also have Monday-kick offs, virtual lunches and fikas, and online gaming groups to keep the workday fun.
