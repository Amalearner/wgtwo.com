The team and your role

Our vision is to create programmable mobile networks to allow our customers and their end-users to create more valuable and useful products and services. We do this by deploying a programmable core network, making it accessible through developer-friendly APIs, and then creating a marketplace where customers and developers can meet. On the journey there we create a consistent, cloud-based, highly efficient core network delivered as-a-service. We know where we‚Äôre going, and with a lot of trust in each other, we‚Äôll pave the way there together.

We are in an exciting expansion phase and in addition to scaling up all our engineering teams we have started to build up a world-class Customer Success team.

So, if you have a passion for customers and tech, this is a great opportunity to join a strong international team. We are looking for a Delivery Manager (or several actually) with excellent communication and interpersonal skills, who know how to build positive and productive working relationships with customers for business growth. You will work in a dedicated Customer Success Team consisting of Project Managers, Program Managers, and Technical Support Engineers.

As our Delivery Manager you will:

  • Drive the delivery of customer integration projects as well as internal roadmap delivery projects that require cross team coordination

  • Ensure projects are delivered in time and with quality

  • Act as a bridge between the customer and internal agile development teams

  • Manage external and internal stakeholder, maintaining communication and aligning expectations

  • Be the face of the project internally and to our customers

  • Prioritization of project backlogs


We believe you have:

  • A solid background with customer facing delivery projects

  • Experience from integration projects, ideally from Telco industry

  • Experience in managing agile development in waterfall delivery projects

  • Experience in mapping HLD documentation to functional requirements for delivery teams

  • Experience with reporting related to project governance 

Terms like IPSec, Sigtran, SS7, Diameter, SIP, BGP, LTE, UMTS, GSM, EPC, IMS should not be foreign to you.
